Site Guide

How to Use This Site

A structured guide for government evaluators, strategic partners, and members of the public navigating MTSAi.org.

For Government Evaluators — The Fast Lane

If you are a procurement officer, city traffic authority, or urban mobility official, follow this path:

01

Understand the System

Read the How the System Works series. Start with "Why Traditional Traffic Management Fails" and follow the series in order.

02

Review Our Trust Posture

Visit the Trust Centre. The Data Governance and Security Posture pages are most relevant for procurement due diligence.

03

Request the Procurement Pack

Use the Contact page to request the Procurement Pack. Select "Government Evaluation" as your intent.

For Strategic Partners

If you are a systems integrator, smart city vendor, or infrastructure provider exploring integration:

01
⬡

Review the Technology Platform

Understand our architecture and the Open Integration Model before reaching out. Visit Technology Platform for technical detail.

02
⇄

Contact via Strategic Partner Intent

Use the Contact page and select "Strategic Partnership" as your intent. Our team reviews partnership enquiries on a rolling basis.

For Members of the Public

This website is an informational resource about a pre-deployment platform. If you have a concern about how traffic management data is collected or used in a pilot, visit the Public Integrity page.

Forms and Simulations

Several interactive features on this site — including simulation dashboards and inline procurement request forms — are not yet live. These will be enabled as the platform moves from build to pilot phase. In the meantime, use the Contact page for all requests.
