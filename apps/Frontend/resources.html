Procurement Pathways

GeM Route (Where Available)

Procurement may be routed via GeM where applicable and where listing/category conditions are met. This website does not imply GeM listing unless explicitly stated in a listing artefact.

Tender Route (CPPP/eProcure)

Procurement may be routed via tendering processes consistent with buyer policy and applicable rules.

All materials are distributed on request — Contact the team to receive your pack →

Tender Support Checklist

✓ Scope boundary (pilot vs scale) and success criteria
✓ Governance owners: Programme, IT/Security, Privacy/Legal, Procurement
✓ Hosting model selection: buyer policy-driven
✓ Integrations list: identity, payments, reporting, enforcement workflows
✓ Required artefacts: packs, runbooks, audit exports, change control
✓ Evaluation outputs: what evidence constitutes acceptance
