Our Journey

From a single vision in Vancouver to a procurement-ready platform serving Indian cities — seven years of deliberate, verifiable progress.

01 / Founded 2019
Company Founded

Vision established in Vancouver, Canada. Research into congestion pricing models and government procurement cycles begins.

02 / R&D 2021
R&D Partnerships

Budapest University of Technology collaboration begins. Occupancy detection feasibility study using Bluetooth and WiFi signals.

03 / Expansion 2023
India Operations

Bangalore HQ established. 50% operational presence committed. ANPR partner Adaptive Recognition integration scoped for India.

04 / Alignment 2024
GoI Alignment

Smart Cities Mission integration mapped. DPDP, CERT-In, and GIGW compliance frameworks embedded into platform architecture.

05 / Now 2026
Pilot Ready

Platform ready for city-wide deployment. Procurement partnerships active. First Tier-2 city evaluation underway.
