Hyperlinking Policy

This policy sets out our position on linking to MTSAi.org and on external links from MTSAi.org.

Linking to MTSAi.org

You are permitted to link to any publicly accessible page on MTSAi.org, subject to the following conditions:

  • The link must not create the impression that MTSAi endorses you, your organisation, or your content
  • The link must not embed our pages within a frame (framing is not permitted)
  • The link text or surrounding context must not misrepresent the nature of MTSAi's platform or services
  • You must not link to MTSAi.org in a way that implies a commercial relationship that does not exist

External Links from MTSAi.org

Where MTSAi.org links to external websites, those links are provided for reference only. MTSAi does not:

  • Endorse the content, products, or services of linked sites
  • Guarantee the accuracy or currency of external content
  • Accept responsibility for the privacy practices of linked sites

Reporting a Linking Concern

If you believe a link to or from MTSAi.org is inappropriate, misleading, or in breach of this policy, please report it via our feedback channel.
