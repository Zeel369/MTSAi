Our Journey

From vision to
pilot-ready.

2019

Company Founded

Vision established in Vancouver, Canada. The core thesis: cities need an incentive-based, not penalty-based, approach to congestion management.

2021

R&D Partnerships

Budapest University of Technology (BME) collaboration begins — occupancy detection innovation using Bluetooth/WiFi signals to support equity-focused pricing models.

2023

India Operations

Bangalore HQ established. India becomes the primary operational hub — government contract entity, India-based team, and GeM/tender compliance framework put in place.

2024

GoI Alignment

Smart Cities Mission integration work begins. Platform architecture aligned to GIGW 3.0, DPDPA 2023, and CERT-In design standards.

2026 · Target

Pilot Ready

Deployment partnerships active. Live corridor trial with a government partner city — the last step, not the first claim.
