Technical Architecture · Government-Grade

Architecture for Government-Controlled Mobility Programmes

The MTSAi platform is designed as a multi-tenant mobility management architecture supporting policy configuration, incentive systems, traffic event processing, and digital ecosystem integrations.

City policy operations — congestion policies, incentive budgets, exemptions, and enforcement thresholds — remain under government control at every layer.

Privacy & Deployment Posture

No continuous GPS tracking. No biometrics. Tenant isolation. Cloud deployment designed for regulated rollout under government oversight.

Privacy Architecture

Privacy is not a compliance layer on top of the platform. It is a constraint that shapes the architecture itself.
